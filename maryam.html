The average temperature of Egypt during the summer months is 104 degrees! It can even be as high as 122 degrees! If one travels there in the winter, the temperature can range from 45-70 degrees, the coldest month being January, with an average temperature of 66 degrees
The offcial language of Eygpt is Arabic. To say hello is "As-Salaam-Alaikum", which means "Peace be upon you", or you can just say "Salam". To say good bye, you can say "Ma'aasalaama". In addition if someone says "As-Salaam-Alaikum", you can repsond by saying "Wa-Alaikum-Salaam", which means "Unto you be Peace".
While the most famous attraction in Egypt is the Pyramids of Giza, located on the outskirts of Cairo, Egypt, there are some other attarctions located in Eygpt, specifically in Cairo, Egypt, that can teach one about Eygptian history. Two examples are The Egyptian Museum-which contains over 5,000 years of Egyptian history- and The Al-Azhar Mosque- oen of the earliest surviving mosques, and one of the world's oldest universities!
